VXDIGITAL’s Rationale

Home Rationale

Currently my organization provides Digital Marketing, Branding, Digital Process Automation, Market Analysis, Marketing Strategy Optimization, Social Media Management, Search Engine Optimization, Search Engine Marketing, Website Development, App Development, Business Registration, Digital Payment Gateways, Event and Business Promotion, Ads Campaign, Brand Identity System. Though I would offer various other digital solutions in the future.

Our primary target is businesses, organizations, corporations, and SMEs.

Our core values are Integrity, Hard Work, Innovation, Excellence, Resilience, Proactive, and Solution Driven.

What differentiates us is our approach. We are determined to help our clients succeed and meet their goals. We show this commitment by listening to our clients, assessing their needs and pain points, and tailoring solutions while delivering results with excellence in both service and delivery.

In the short term, we hope to grow to be a hub for tech talents, delivering projects and becoming renowned as the leading digital marketing agency across the Southeastern Region. We hope to grow our clientele.

The impact we want to have includes a community and hub for tech talents to gain hands-on experience, successfully helping thousands of entrepreneurs and organizations grow their businesses, and raising the standards of service delivery and innovation in the digital marketing space.

In 5-10 years, we envision being one of the leading tech companies in the country, having expanded from a digital marketing agency to include child companies specializing in app development, IT infrastructure, and a community for tech talents and mentors. We aim to have trained 1,000 enterprising tech talents.

We want to create solutions to actual problems businesses and organizations face, develop software and applications that solve real issues for individuals, and build a self-sustaining, resilient, and independent IT infrastructure for Africa.

Challenges include getting the right team members and lead generation.

Opportunities include AI simplifying our work processes, remote work reducing costs, and social media increasing awareness of the importance of digital marketing efforts.

The type of work culture we want to foster includes everyone feeling valued and respected, the ability to work independently and as a team, a collaborative and teachable environment, proactivity, innovation, creativity, and delivering excellent results within timelines while making good decisions under pressure.

I want our employees and customers to feel that we value them, we respect them, we listen and care about them, we understand and can relate to their needs. We want them to feel like they belong, we are part of their team, always rooting for them, and we are committed to their success.
